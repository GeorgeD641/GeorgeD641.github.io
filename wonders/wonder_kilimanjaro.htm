IN CIVILIZATION 6

Mount Kilimanjaro is a single-tile impassable Natural Wonder in Civilization VI. Mount Kilimanjaro occupies a Desert, Grassland, Plains, or Tundra tile, and provides +2 Food and +2 Appeal to each adjacent tile.
It is considered a Mountain in-game. In Civilization VI: Rise and Fall, discovering Mount Kilimanjaro grants +1 Era Score, or +3 Score if the player is the first to do so. In Civilization VI: Gathering Storm, Mount Kilimanjaro is considered a Volcano and can thus erupt, damaging and providing yields to nearby tiles.


THE AWE OF AFRICA

Mount Kilimanjaro or just Kilimanjaro with its three volcanic cones, Kibo, Mawenzi, and Shira, is a dormant volcano in Tanzania.
It is the highest mountain in Africa, with its summit about 4,900 metres (16,100 ft) from its base, and 5,895 metres (19,341 ft) above sea level.
The first people known to have reached the summit of the mountain were Hans Meyer and Ludwig Purtscheller, in 1889.
The mountain is part of Kilimanjaro National Park and is a major climbing destination.
The mountain has been the subject of many scientific studies because of its shrinking glaciers and disappearing ice fields.
